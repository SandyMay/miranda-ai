Privacy Policy

I built Miranda AI as an experience for people that love fashion and love the character that this app was inspired by and I care about being straightforward with you. Here's exactly what happens with your data.

What I collect

  • Basic usage data through Vercel Analytics: page views, device type, browser, and country. This is aggregate and anonymized — I can't identify you from it.
  • Vercel, my hosting provider, may log standard request metadata (IP address, timestamp, status code) for up to 30 days as part of normal infrastructure operation.

What I don't collect

  • No personal profile.
  • No account or login.
  • No cookies beyond what's essential for the app to function.
  • No targeted ads.
  • No selling of data. Ever.

Your photos

This is the important one. When you upload a photo, it is sent directly to Anthropic's API (the AI that powers Miranda's responses) and is not stored anywhere — not by me, not on any server I control. Once you receive your verdict, the photo is gone. I never see it, I never save it, I never have access to it.

Your verdict

Verdicts are not stored on any server. When you share a verdict, the text is encoded directly in the URL. Anyone with that link can read it, so only share it if you're comfortable with that.

Third-party services

  • Anthropic — processes your photo and generates the verdict. Their privacy policy applies to how they handle API requests: anthropic.com/privacy
  • Vercel — hosts the app and the serverless function. Their privacy policy: vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

Age

Miranda AI is intended for users 13 and older.
